What About Sigale gale ?

Sigale - gale is one of the most iconic historical objects of the Batak people in Samosir Island was created by a king ages ago, the king was very sad by rejecting the fact that his son was dead so he was creating the identic-shaped puppet that looks like his son and asked the spirit to possess the puppet so that it looks alive

What Lake Toba ?

Lake Toba (Indonesian: Danau Toba) (Batak Toba: Tao Toba) is a large natural lake in North Sumatra, Indonesia, occupying the caldera of a supervolcano. The lake is located in the middle of the northern part of the island of Sumatra, with a surface elevation of about 900 metres (2,953 ft), the lake stretches from WikiMiniAtlas2.88°N 98.52°E to 2.35°N 99.1°E
